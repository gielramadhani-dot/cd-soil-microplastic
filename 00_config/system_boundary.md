# System boundary

Article 1 focuses only on soil-level cadmium mobilization under microplastic co-contamination.

Included:
- microplastic type
- microplastic aging / oxidation
- Cd–microplastic molecular interaction
- pH
- organic matter
- CEC
- clay fraction
- Cd mobilization tendency
- soil pollution regime classification

Excluded:
- plant uptake
- crop transfer factor
- edible Cd concentration
- dietary exposure
- hazard quotient
- human risk
- field validation

Working title:
Scenario-Based Computational Modeling of Microplastic-Aging Effects on Cadmium Mobilization in Agricultural Soils

STAGE 10C NETWORK AND ALLUVIAL PATHWAY ANALYSIS REPORT
================================================================================

Purpose:
This stage creates network and alluvial analyses to show how microplastic identity,
MRI class, soil geochemical class, Cd loading, and CMI regimes are connected.

Alluvial pathway:
fragment_condition → MRI_class → Msoil_class → Cd_loading_class → CMI_regime

Network pathway:
Edges represent aggregated scenario flows between categorical states.

Sensitivity network:
Edges represent permutation-importance-based links from mechanistic controls to CMI.

They do not replace the CMI and sensitivity results but help show how molecular
microplastic descriptors and soil geochemistry jointly route scenarios into final
Cd mobilization regimes.

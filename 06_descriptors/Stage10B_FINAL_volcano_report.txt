STAGE 10B-FINAL POINT-LEVEL VOLCANO REPORT
================================================================================

Purpose:
This final version creates a volcano-style CMI enrichment landscape that visually resembles
a standard volcano plot while remaining adapted to scenario-based Cd mobilization modeling.

Point definition:
Each point represents one sampled CMI scenario.

x-axis:
log2 fold change of CMI relative to the matched median CMI.
Matching was based on soil pH, organic matter, particle size, and Cd total level.

y-axis:
Capped -log10 q-like evidence score derived from standardized deviation within matched scenario backgrounds.

Thresholds:
log2FC threshold = ±0.55
q-like threshold = 0.05
Y-axis cap = 6.5

Class counts:
- not strongly differentiated: 179987
- reduced mobilization risk: 13

Interpretation caution:
This is a volcano-style scenario enrichment plot, not a gene-expression volcano plot.
The y-axis is an evidence score for modeled scenario separation, not an experimental p-value.
